MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Mechanical engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It is one of the oldest and broadest engineering disciplines, encompassing various aspects of physics, materials science, mathematics, and computer science. Mechanical engineers apply scientific principles and technical knowledge to create practical solutions for a wide range of industries and technologies.
